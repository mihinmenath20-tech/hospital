about us

The Hospital Directory and Doctor Portal are essential components of the CityMed Hospital Management System. The Hospital Directory maintains comprehensive information about all hospital departments and medical staff, allowing patients and administrators to easily access details such as doctor names, specialties, contact information, and department locations. The Doctor Portal provides a secure platform for doctors to manage their daily activities efficiently. Through the portal, doctors can view patient records, manage appointments, update diagnoses, prescribe medications, and access patient medical histories. These features help improve communication, streamline healthcare services, reduce administrative workload, and ensure that patients receive timely and effective medical care.
